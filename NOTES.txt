
GAME FLOW:
- Choose goal: certain relationships with certain characters, by a certain event/time
	- Pre-made story situations
	- Randomly generated
- Player joins chatroom. Other characters join and leave periodically. They chat with each other and when they address the player, the player has several buttons to respond with.
	- Agree/Disagree/Neutral
	- Laugh/Compliment
	- Ignore
	- Insult
	- Mention other character
- Each response has an effect on all relevant characters in the chatroom. Characters with close relationships may also pass on information to other characters outside of the chatroom.
- Game follows a timeline with events happening at intervals. Player must reach relationship goals by the deadline.
